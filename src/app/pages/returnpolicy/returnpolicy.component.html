Cancellation of Orders

In the event you have placed an order and wish to cancel it before the shipment has been dispatched, please notify our customer service team on WhatsApp via 8744012053/8744012036 or via email on amit@petsworld.in. If the order is prepaid, a refund then will be processed to the original mode of payment. Please note that once an order has been dispatched, we cannot cancel the order. You can choose to accept the delivery or refuse the delivery while the delivery process. Once the order has been returned to us and is prepaid, the courier charges will be deducted from the billable amount while refunding the amount.
